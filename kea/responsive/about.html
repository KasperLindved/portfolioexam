About

Kasper Lindved Jensen.

I am a 21 year old, Danish designer, currently studying Multimedia Design at Copenhagen School of Design

I have always been very fond of the creative way of thinking. Doing things differently, and expressing feelings and thoughts visually and audibly. I've been doodling since I can remember, however only when utterly bored. I began playing the piano at 10 and guitar at 13. That was the time, I truly felt that being creative was my thing. Not only because I enjoyed it, but because I felt it came naturally. Later I became very enthusiastic with making short films, and playing around with visual effects. That made me choose a cinematics course in high school, and I absolutely loved it. I loved every aspect of producing an artwork, and showcase it to an audience and recieve the feedback and criticism.

I took two years off after high school, and this free time helped me a lot to figure out, what I really wanted to do. Initially, I thought about appyling to the Copenhagen Film School, and was supposed to start after my first year off. However, as I began drawing for a local caf√©, I realized that I didn't want to specify my creative skills to much. I want to make movies as much as I want to draw on blackboards and walls, make music and webdesign. This is where KEA (Copenhagen School of Design) came in the picture, as they have a Multimedia Designer course. Despite the fact, that I've only been here for 3 weeks as I write this, I honestly feel that it's a perfect choice. Design-wise, I really dig the old-fashioned jazzy style and Art Deco. Nevertheless, I also have a very urban style, inspired by todays Hip Hop culture.
