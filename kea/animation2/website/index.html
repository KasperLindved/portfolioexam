Yojimbo

The adventure!

Yojimbo, the Adventure! is a graphic novel that explores the incredible adventures of Yojimbo the Samuray.
Yojimbo travels for several days untill he is able to find a nice town where a history of love, trust, friendship, lies and adventures awaits from him.

Yojimbo is a loner, a traveler. Wondering around, he finds himself in this lost town where two gangs have control of almost everything.

Yojimbo decides to double play both sides os the conflict. He gains the trust of each gang to finally make them stand on each other.

But the gangs find out what he is doing. And decide to kill Yojimbo.
Yojimbo must find himself of this conflict on an interesting battle, where the best insult will bring him a successfull winning and a safe exit of all this mess, while at the same time he will be the heroe that will bring peace and joy to this incredible town.
